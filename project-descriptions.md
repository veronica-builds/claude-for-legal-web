# Claude Project Setup: Names and Descriptions

When creating each project on claude.ai, use the following values for the "What are you working on?" and "What are you trying to achieve?" fields.

---

**Commercial Legal**
- Name: `Commercial Legal Assistant`
- Description: Support the commercial legal team with contract review, NDA triage, vendor agreement tracking, risk assessment, and meeting prep.

---

**Privacy & Data Protection**
- Name: `Privacy & Data Protection Assistant`
- Description: Support the privacy team with DPA review, data subject rights responses, privacy impact assessments, and multi-jurisdiction compliance checks under GDPR, CCPA, and related regulations.

---

**Employment Legal**
- Name: `Employment Legal Assistant`
- Description: Support the employment legal team with termination risk review, workplace investigation workflows, FMLA/ADA leave tracking, offer letter and restrictive covenant review, and policy drafting across jurisdictions.

---

**Litigation**
- Name: `Litigation Assistant`
- Description: Support the litigation team with matter intake, legal hold notices, privilege analysis, demand letter triage, and factual chronology building.

---

**IP Legal**
- Name: `IP Legal Assistant`
- Description: Support the IP team with trademark clearance analysis, freedom-to-operate triage, OSS license review, and cease and desist drafting and response.

---

**AI Governance**
- Name: `AI Governance Assistant`
- Description: Support the AI governance team with EU AI Act risk tier classification, AI impact assessments, vendor AI contract review, and regulatory gap analysis for new AI regulations.

---

**Regulatory**
- Name: `Regulatory Assistant`
- Description: Support the regulatory practice group with gap analysis between new regulations and client policies, proposed policy redrafts, NPRM comment period tracking, and regulatory comment letter drafting.
